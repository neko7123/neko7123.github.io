  • About

    Welcome to the 15th edition of TechChase, the ultimate fusion of fun and engineering, organized by the Student Council of Viva Institute of Technology, Shirgaon, Virar (E). Since our establishment in 2009, we've grown into a vibrant community where innovation meets enjoyment, and this year's theme, "Yugantar: A Leap into the Future," takes that spirit to new heights.

    Join us at TechChase 2024, where we combine the excitement of games with the essence of engineering, making this a leap into the future you won't want to miss!
